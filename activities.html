Austin Kayak Activities & Paddle Routes

Explore the best paddling spots on Town Lake and Lake Austin. We deliver your kayak directly to the ramp.

Downtown Town Lake Skyline

Launch from Redbud Trail or Texas Rowing Center and paddle past the Austin skyline, Congress Avenue Bridge, and the famous bat colony (summer evenings).

Lake Austin Scenic Paddle

From Walsh Boat Landing or Emma Long Park, paddle calm waters with limestone cliffs, herons, turtles, and quiet coves.

Fishing Kayak Spots

Barton Creek pool and Festival Beach are known for bass and sunfish. Our fishing kayaks include rod holders.

Sunset Paddle

Book a late afternoon delivery to Loop 360 Ramp and watch the sun set over the hills.

Delivery to any ramp

You choose the put-in spot. We bring the kayaks, paddles, and life vests. You return them to the same ramp.
