How Kayak Delivery Works

1. Choose Your Ramp

Select from our list of Town Lake or Lake Austin boat ramps, or give us GPS coordinates.

2. Pick Date & Time

Reserve your preferred start time. We deliver 30 minutes before your rental begins.

3. We Deliver

Our team drops off kayaks, paddles, and life vests at the ramp. You paddle and return.

4. We Pick Up

Leave the kayaks at the ramp. No hauling, no strapping — we handle everything.
